My Hero - Sun Yingsha

Sun Yingsha or known as Shasha, is the world's number 1 table tennis player for women’s singles since 2022 until now. She is a right-handed player with a shakehand grip style. She is known by her exceptional speed and precision. She also recognized for her resilience, strategic approach, and determination to triumph over the toughest competitors.

Career

2019
In the 2019 World Team Cup, Sun Yingsha came from behind 10–7 in the final game to beat Mima Ito and win against Japan.
2021
At the start of 2021, Sun Yingsha was ranked second in the world.
2023
On October 1, Sun won the women’s singles gold at the Hangzhou Asian Games, defeating Japan’s Hina Hayata 4-1.
2024
At the 2024 Paris Olympics, Sun Yingsha won gold in mixed doubles, gold in women’s team, and silver in women’s singles.
