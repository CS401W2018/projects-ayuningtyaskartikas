Here is the family members

Bill Andersen

Bill Andersen is a dedicated father and husband, with family as his top priority. He is a hardworking and disciplined man, but he gets frustrated easily when things don't go according to plan. Although he is quick to show his anger, he is also understanding and takes the time to comfort his daughter, Riley, when she is upset. Bill is a man who truly cares about his family, and is also funny.

Riley Andersen

Riley used to be a happy and silly child, but as she entered her pre-teen years, she began to feel more insecure and inferior. Her move to San Francisco made her feel even lonelier, and she found it difficult to deal with her emotions. The change was overwhelming, but over time, she learned to open up and express her emotions to her family, which eventually helped her accept the changes in her life..

Jill Andersen

Jill is a loving and caring mother, always looking out for her family. Although she can be frustrated by her husband's cluelessness, she still loves him and tries her best to support her daughter, Riley. Jill's compassion and ability to empathize with others comes from her deep understanding of emotions, perhaps shaped by her own experiences. She is always there for the people she loves.
