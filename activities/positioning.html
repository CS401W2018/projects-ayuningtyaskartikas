  • What is normal flow in HTML?

    • >> The main elements demand more space of the page than the other. There is an order vertically (example H2 is higher than p tag).

  • What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?

    • >> Float takes an element out of the normal flow, it can align to left or right. We need clear the float to prevent elements from overlapping.

  • What do absolute, fixed, and relative do?

    • >> Absolute (you can still scroll it, but it's consistent, example : button for live chat on the right below of an website). an element relative to its nearest positioned ancestor, fixed the elements doesn't scroll (it's just stay there, example: the header on the top of the website). The relative moves an element relative to its normal position.
